Carpentry Companion (AU)

Carpentry Companion (AU) is a practical study and planning app for builders, apprentices, and renovators who want fast, reliable tools on-site and offline.

Support Privacy Policy

What it includes

  • Core calculators for common carpentry and framing scenarios
  • Job-based workflows to organize notes, values, and saved references
  • Export options for sharing and backup
  • Offline-first usage so your essential data is available without signal

Availability

Designed for iPhone, iPad, and Mac (Apple platforms), with synced purchase handling through Apple infrastructure.
