Product Overview

GiftNest

Remember thoughtful gifts before you need them. GiftNest keeps people, occasions, gift ideas, photos, notes, and clean store links in one calm, local-first app.

At a Glance

Local-first

Gift lists, notes, and images stay on device in the current app build.

Optional

Contacts import is read-only and user initiated.

Clean links

Saved gift links remove common tracking and outside affiliate parameters.

iOS

Built for iPhone and iPad gift planning workflows.

Built for Thoughtful Gift Planning

People profiles

Save birthdays, relationships, interests, sizes, favorite colors, notes, and optional photos.

Gift ideas

Track ideas by person, occasion, price, priority, and status from first thought through given.

Occasions

Keep upcoming birthdays, holidays, and special dates visible before they become urgent.

Fast capture

Capture gift ideas with notes and photos when you see something worth remembering.

Examples that get out of the way

Example data is clearly labeled and disappears when real people or gifts are added.

Affiliate-aware links

GiftNest can use configured GiftNest affiliate links for supported stores, with disclosure.

Privacy Approach

No account required

The current app build does not require a GiftNest account or backend login.

Read-only Contacts import

GiftNest reads only names, birthdays, thumbnail photos, and local contact IDs when the user approves import.

No third-party ad SDKs

The current app build does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
