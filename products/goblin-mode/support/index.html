Common questions

Does Goblin Mode require an account?

No. Goblin Mode is designed to work locally on your iPhone.

Does the advisor require AI or an API key?

No. The Goblin Advisor is rule-based and works without network services.

How do I remove my data?

Delete the app from your device to remove locally stored app data. If the app includes an in-app reset control, you can also use that to return to the default sample state.

Is Goblin Mode advice?

No. It provides small productivity prompts. It is not a medical, mental health, nutrition, financial, legal, or safety service.
