Privacy Policy

Goblin Mode is local-first. The app is designed to work without accounts, subscriptions, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, backend services, API keys, or cloud AI services.

Information the app stores

Goblin Mode may store app data locally on your device so the app can remember your progress and preferences. This can include:

  • Daily check-in choices such as energy, hunger, mood, weather, money caution, and available time.
  • Quest state, active quests, completed quests, loot, coins, scraps, trinkets, achievements, and titles.
  • Hoard, deal/pass, food, cave care, route, inventory, settings, and onboarding preferences.

Data collection

The native app does not collect personal data for the developer, does not use tracking, and does not send app usage data to a server.

Data sharing

Goblin Mode does not sell, rent, or share your app data with advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or third-party AI services.

Local storage and deletion

App data is stored locally by iOS for the Goblin Mode app. You can delete local app data by deleting the app from your device. If a reset option is available in the app, it will replace local state with the default sample state.

Permissions

The current app is designed to run without requiring sensitive system permissions such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, HealthKit, or tracking permission.

Children

Goblin Mode is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Professional advice

Goblin Mode is a productivity and self-management tool. It is not medical, mental health, nutrition, financial, legal, or safety advice. Use your own judgment and seek qualified professional help when needed.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.
