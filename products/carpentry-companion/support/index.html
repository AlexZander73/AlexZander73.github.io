FAQ

  • How do I restore purchases?
    Open the app and use the restore purchases option in subscription/settings. Ensure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
  • How do trial and pro access work?
    Trial and paid access are managed through Apple billing. Pro features unlock automatically when entitlement is active.
  • Is data stored offline and can I back it up?
    Yes. Data is stored locally on your device. Use in-app export/backup options to create and restore backups.
  • How do I report an issue?
    Email index-hearty6c@icloud.com with device model, iOS/iPadOS/macOS version, app version, and reproduction steps.
